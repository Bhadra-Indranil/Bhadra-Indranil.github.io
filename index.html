My interests lie at the intersection of deep learning, computer vision, and scientific computing, with a focus on research-driven applications. From Behaviour to Concepts: Safety Vulnerabilities in LLMs

Independent Research

Uncovered that models are more prone to sycophancy in harassment contexts vs. cybercrime. TSP using Simulated Annealing

IIT Guwahati | C++

Tackled the Traveling Salesman Problem, a complex combinatorial optimization challenge, aiming to minimize travel distance among multiple cities. Explored optimization techniques and identified Simulated Annealing as a suitable method due to its effectiveness in finding near-optimal solutions within feasible computational limits. Developed the algorithm in C++, implementing the Simulated Annealing approach with custom parameter tuning for temperature and cooling rate to enhance performance. Achieved a near-optimal solution with minimized travel path, outperforming brute-force alternatives in both speed and accuracy.
